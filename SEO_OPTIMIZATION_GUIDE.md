### Step 1: Google Search Console Setup (Critical)
**Why:** This tells Google your site exists and helps it get indexed faster.

1. **Go to Google Search Console**
   - Visit: https://search.google.com/search-console
   - Sign in with your Google account

2. **Add Property**
   - Click "Add Property"
   - Enter: `https://arivlabs.com`
   - Choose "URL prefix" method

3. **Verify Ownership**
   - Method 1 (Recommended): HTML file upload
     - Download the verification file
     - Upload it to your Netlify site's public folder
     - Redeploy your site
   
   - Method 2: HTML tag
     - Add the meta tag to your `index.html` head section
     - Redeploy your site

4. **Submit Sitemap**
   - Once verified, go to "Sitemaps" in the left menu
   - Enter: `https://arivlabs.com/sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"

5. **Request Indexing**
   - Go to "URL Inspection" tool
   - Enter: `https://arivlabs.com`
   - Click "Request Indexing"
