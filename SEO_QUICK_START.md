## üöÄ Immediate Actions (Do This Today!)

### 1. Google Search Console - MOST IMPORTANT (15 minutes)
**This is the #1 most critical step to get your site indexed by Google.**

**Steps:**
1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Click "Start Now" and sign in with your Google account
3. Click "Add Property" ‚Üí Select "URL prefix"
4. Enter: `https://arivlabs.com`
5. **Verify ownership using HTML tag method:**
   - Copy the meta tag Google provides
   - It will look like: `<meta name="google-site-verification" content="YOUR_CODE_HERE" />`
   - I can add this to your index.html if you provide it
   - OR manually add it to the `<head>` section of your index.html
6. After verification, go to "Sitemaps" in the left sidebar
7. Enter: `sitemap.xml` (just the filename)
8. Click "Submit"
9. Go to "URL Inspection" tool (magnifying glass icon at top)
10. Type: `https://arivlabs.com` and press Enter
11. Click "Request Indexing" button

**Expected Result:** Google will crawl your site within 24-48 hours
